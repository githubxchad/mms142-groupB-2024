Wearable Devices in the Health Industry

Introduction

Wearable devices have rapidly become a key part of the health industry, transforming how individuals monitor and manage their health on a daily basis. These devices, such as smartwatches, fitness trackers, and wearable medical monitors, enable users to track vital signs, physical activity, and other health metrics in real-time.

In this discussion, we will explore topics such as what defines disruptive technologies, the role of wearable devices, its impact on traditional healthcare methods, and how they are reshaping patient care and expectations. We will also examine market shifts, benefits, and challenges that come with this technological revolution in healthcare.
